My Background

Driven by curiosity, I enjoy turning ideas into playable experiences.

I’m Pratyush Gaur, a game developer who believes in learning by building. For me, development isn’t just about writing code it’s about creating interactive experiences that feel real, engaging, and meaningful.

I work with Unity and Unreal Engine 5 to design gameplay systems, experiment with mechanics, and bring concepts to life. Alongside this, I use Blender to create and integrate props, Sometimes this is part of a project pipeline, and sometimes it’s simply for fun, bringing ideas and imagination into 3D space.

Through college projects, solo development, and hands-on experimentation, I’ve learned the importance of ownership, clarity, and teamwork in building polished games. value thoughtful design, long-term thinking, and creating systems that scale as projects grow

I enjoy exploring how games work beneath the surface whether it’s gameplay logic, performance optimization, or system design. I focus on writing clean, maintainable code and improving through iteration, playtesting, and constant learning rather than quick fixes.

Beyond development, I value balance and personal growth. Gym keeps me disciplined, music helps me focus, and walking and running give me space to think and reset. I enjoy playing games to study mechanics from a player’s perspective, and cooking as a creative outlet that teaches patience and experimentation. My goal is to keep growing as a game developer, contribute to impactful projects, and build experiences that players genuinely enjoy.
